About Us

Forlate Cargo Solution

has been a trusted leader in the transportation industry since 2006, delivering reliable and efficient freight solutions. We specialize in full truckload (FTL) deliveries across South Africa, consolidated or part-load shipments to major cities, and cross-border logistics to key destinations across Southern Africa. With decades of expertise, a modern fleet, and a commitment to excellence, we provide tailored logistics solutions that prioritize efficiency, safety, and on-time delivery. Our cross-border services ensure seamless international transportation, navigating customs regulations and border clearance with precision. Whether you require large-scale freight transport, flexible cargo consolidation, or reliable international logistics, Forlate Cargo Solutions is your trusted partner in supply chain management.
